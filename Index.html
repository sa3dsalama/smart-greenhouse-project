Tools and Sensors We Use

Soil Moisture Sensor

This sensor measures the moisture level in the soil, ensuring the plants get the right amount of water for optimal growth.

ESP32 Board

The ESP32 is the brain of the system, used for controlling the sensors, processing data, and managing the entire greenhouse automation system.

Water Pump Motor

Controls water flow to the plants, ensuring they receive the proper amount of water according to the soil moisture data.

Ultrasonic Water Level Sensor

Measures the water level in the tank, helping us maintain an adequate water supply for irrigation and prevent overflow.

Camera for Disease Detection

Used to capture images of plants and analyze for signs of disease, allowing us to take early action in preventing plant health issues.

Heater (Lamp)

Maintains a warm environment for plants during colder months, helping to regulate the temperature for optimal growth conditions.

Motor to Open Window

This motor automatically opens or closes the greenhouse windows based on temperature and humidity conditions, providing better air circulation.

Fan Cooler

Used to cool down the greenhouse in hot weather, maintaining a comfortable environment for plants and improving air circulation.
